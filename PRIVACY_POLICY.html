Privacy Policy for TapWake

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to TapWake! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our alarm application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, and profile picture (if you choose to provide one)
  • Authentication Data: Credentials used for Google Sign-In
  • User ID: Unique identifier assigned to your account

2.2 Audio Data

  • Tap Sound Recordings: Audio recordings of your tap water sound used for alarm dismissal
  • Storage: All audio recordings are stored locally on your device and in your private Firebase storage
  • Processing: Audio is only used for comparing sounds during alarm dismissal

2.3 Usage Data

  • Alarm Data: Alarm times, labels, repeat schedules, and dismissal history
  • Streak Data: Daily streak tracking, longest streak, and completion dates
  • TapCoins & Purchases: In-app currency balance, purchase history, and owned items
  • Social Data: Friend connections, quest participation, leaderboard rankings, and messages
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version

2.4 Advertising Data

  • Ad Interactions: Information about ads you view and interact with
  • Google AdMob: Google may collect additional data through AdMob (see Google's Privacy Policy)

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Functions

  • Provide alarm functionality and tap sound verification
  • Manage your user account and authentication
  • Track streaks, achievements, and rewards
  • Enable social features (friends, quests, leaderboards)
  • Process in-app purchases and TapCoin transactions

3.2 Improvements & Analytics

  • Analyze app usage to improve features and user experience
  • Debug issues and fix technical problems
  • Monitor app performance and stability

3.3 Advertising

  • Display relevant ads through Google AdMob
  • Reward users for watching ads (TapCoins, streak maintenance)

4. Data Storage & Security

4.1 Where We Store Your Data

  • Local Storage: Audio recordings, alarm settings, and preferences stored on your device
  • Firebase Cloud: User accounts, social data, and backup information stored securely in Google Firebase
  • Geographic Location: Data may be stored on servers in various countries where Firebase operates

4.2 Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption for data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Firebase Authentication for secure account access
  • Regular security updates and vulnerability monitoring
  • Access controls limiting who can access your data
Important: While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

5.1 We Share Your Data With:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, database, storage, and messaging services
  • Google AdMob: For serving advertisements (see Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Friends on TapWake: Username, streak, and leaderboard data visible to friends you add

5.2 We DO NOT:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Share your audio recordings with anyone
  • Use your data for purposes other than those described in this policy
  • Send your data to marketing companies or data brokers

6. Your Rights & Choices

6.1 Access & Control

  • View Your Data: Access your profile, settings, and data within the app
  • Update Information: Edit your display name and profile information
  • Delete Recordings: Delete your tap sound recordings at any time
  • Manage Friends: Add or remove friends from your social network

6.2 Account Deletion

  • Delete your account anytime through Settings â†’ Delete Account
  • Upon deletion, we will remove your personal data from our active systems
  • Some data may be retained for legal or operational purposes (backups, legal compliance)
  • Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone

6.3 Opt-Out Options

  • Notifications: Disable push notifications in Settings
  • Personalized Ads: Opt out through your device's ad settings
  • Social Features: You can use TapWake without adding friends

7. Children's Privacy

TapWake is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us, and we will delete it immediately.

8. International Users

If you are accessing TapWake from outside your country of residence, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in countries where Firebase servers are located. By using TapWake, you consent to this transfer.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • Sending a notification through the app (for major changes)

  • Legitimate Interests: Improving our app and preventing fraud
  • Consent: For advertising and optional features
  • Legal Obligations: When required by law

  • Request erasure ("right to be forgotten")
